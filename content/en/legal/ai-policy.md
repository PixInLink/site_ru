    <h1 class="use-text-title use-text-primary mb-2">Acceptable Use Policy</h1>
    <p class="use-text-subtitle2 mb-6">FONII LLC &middot; June 19, 2026</p>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">1. About This Document</h2>
      <p>PixInLink gives you a powerful tool — AI image generation. With great power comes responsibility: use the service honestly, lawfully, and with respect for others.</p>
      <p>This document outlines the rules for what you can and cannot create with PixInLink. The rules are based on Russian Federation law, our own policies, and the requirements of <strong>GigaChat API</strong> (Sberbank PJSC) — the technology that powers the service.</p>
      <p>Violating these rules leads to restricted or blocked access. In serious cases, we are required to report information to law enforcement.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4" style="background:var(--color-surface);border-left:4px solid var(--color-accent)">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">2. Prohibited Content</h2>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.1. Illegal Content</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not generate images related to illegal activities.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Terrorist or extremist imagery</li>
          <li>Promotion of drugs, weapons, or explosives</li>
          <li>Forgery of documents — e.g., "passport with a photo" or "driver's license"</li>
          <li>Instructions for committing crimes</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.2. Adult Content (NSFW)</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not generate pornographic or sexually explicit images.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Pornography and sexually explicit content</li>
          <li>Any images involving minors in a sexual context</li>
        </ul>
        <p style="font-size:14px;color:#c62828;margin-bottom:0">Generating materials involving sexualization of minors is an especially serious violation. The account is blocked immediately and permanently; information is reported to law enforcement.</p>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.3. Violence and Cruelty</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not create images depicting or glorifying violence.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Scenes of violence, torture, or self-harm</li>
          <li>Images that glorify or incite violence</li>
          <li>Realistic images of injuries used for intimidation</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.4. Hate Speech</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not generate images that degrade or insult people based on their identity.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Offensive caricatures based on race, nationality, or ethnicity</li>
          <li>Content degrading people based on religion or gender identity</li>
          <li>Images featuring symbols of organizations designated as extremist</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.5. Copyright Infringement</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not copy others' intellectual property without permission.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Generating well-known characters — "Mickey Mouse in an office," "Spider-Man on Red Square"</li>
          <li>Copying company logos and branding — "a logo just like Apple's, but with a different name"</li>
          <li>Creating images of celebrities in contexts they have not consented to</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.6. Deception and Disinformation</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not create images that mislead people.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Generating a fake "on-scene photo" and presenting it as real</li>
          <li>Creating fake chat screenshots, documents, or news headlines</li>
          <li>Using celebrity images in a false context — e.g., "a well-known politician endorsing a dubious product"</li>
        </ul>
      </div>

      <div style="margin-bottom:20px">
        <h3 class="mb-2" style="font-size:16px;color:#c62828">2.7. Spam and Service Abuse</h3>
        <p class="mb-1">Do not use technical methods to bypass limits or overload the service.</p>
        <p class="mb-1"><strong>Not allowed:</strong></p>
        <ul>
          <li>Creating many accounts to bypass free plan limits</li>
          <li>Using automated scripts for mass image generation</li>
          <li>Flooding the service with requests that disrupt other users</li>
        </ul>
      </div>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4" style="background:#f3f8ff;border:1px solid #bbdefb">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">3. Free Plan — Special Rules</h2>
      <p class="mb-2">The Free plan runs on <strong>GigaChat API</strong> (Sberbank PJSC) and carries additional restrictions beyond the general rules.</p>

      <p class="mb-1"><strong>Personal non-commercial use only.</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>Do not use images for profit: selling, advertising, paid courses and newsletters, commercial websites</li>
        <li>You may use them in a personal blog (without ads), social media, and educational projects</li>
      </ul>

      <p class="mb-1"><strong>Watermark is mandatory.</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>All Free images include a "Made with PixInLink" watermark</li>
        <li>Do not remove, crop, paint over, or conceal the watermark in any way</li>
      </ul>

      <p class="mb-0">Commercial use requires a paid plan (Starter / Pro / Business / Enterprise). See <a href="/legal/tos/">Terms of Service</a> for details.</p>
    </div>
