    <h1 class="use-text-title use-text-primary mb-2">Public Offer Agreement</h1>
    <p class="use-text-subtitle2 mb-6">FONII LLC &middot; Revision dated June 19, 2026</p>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <p class="mb-0">This document constitutes an official proposal (public offer) by <strong>FONII Limited Liability Company</strong> (hereinafter — the Contractor) to enter into an agreement for AI image generation services on the terms set forth below.</p>
      <p class="mt-2 mb-0">In accordance with <strong>Article 437 of the Civil Code of the Russian Federation</strong>, this document is a public offer. Acceptance of the offer occurs upon registration on the website and/or payment for services. From the moment of acceptance, the agreement is deemed concluded.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">1. Terms and Definitions</h2>
      <p><strong>Contractor</strong> — FONII LLC, providing services via the PixInLink platform.</p>
      <p><strong>Customer</strong> — an individual or legal entity that has accepted this offer.</p>
      <p><strong>Service</strong> — the PixInLink software and hardware complex, accessible at:</p>
      <ul>
        <li><a href="https://pixinlink.ru">https://pixinlink.ru</a> — informational website and public URL API</li>
        <li><a href="https://app.pixinlink.ru">https://app.pixinlink.ru</a> — user dashboard</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>Service (offering)</strong> — generation of images based on textual descriptions (prompts) using artificial intelligence models and providing access to generated images via URL and CDN.</p>
      <p><strong>Prompt</strong> — a textual description submitted by the Customer for image generation.</p>
      <p><strong>Dashboard</strong> — a secure section of the Service accessible after registration and authentication.</p>
      <p><strong>Plan</strong> — a set of service parameters (number of images per month, watermark presence, limits) purchased by the Customer on a recurring or one-time payment basis.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">2. Subject of the Agreement</h2>
      <p><strong>2.1.</strong> The Contractor undertakes to provide the Customer with image generation services in accordance with the selected Plan, and the Customer undertakes to pay for these services in the manner and on the terms defined by this Agreement.</p>
      <p><strong>2.2.</strong> The list and specifications of Plans are published at <a href="/pricing/">https://pixinlink.ru/pricing/</a> and form an integral part of this Agreement.</p>
      <p><strong>2.3.</strong> The service is deemed rendered at the moment the image is generated and placed on the content delivery network (CDN). A certificate of completion is not drawn up — the fact of image generation is confirmed by a record in the Customer's Dashboard.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">3. Acceptance and Conclusion of the Agreement</h2>
      <p><strong>3.1.</strong> This offer is deemed accepted by the Customer upon the occurrence of any of the following actions:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Registration of an account at <a href="https://app.pixinlink.ru/register">https://app.pixinlink.ru/register</a>;</li>
        <li>Payment for a Plan or service;</li>
        <li>Actual use of the Service (submitting a prompt, receiving an image).</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>3.2.</strong> Acceptance of the offer signifies the Customer's full and unconditional acceptance of all terms of this Agreement.</p>
      <p><strong>3.3.</strong> The Agreement is effective from the moment of acceptance until the parties have fully performed their obligations.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">4. Rights and Obligations of the Contractor</h2>

      <p class="mb-2"><strong>The Contractor undertakes:</strong></p>
      <p><strong>4.1.</strong> To provide image generation services in accordance with the Plan selected by the Customer.</p>
      <p><strong>4.2.</strong> To ensure Service availability at no less than 99.5% monthly uptime (excluding scheduled maintenance, of which the Customer is notified via the Dashboard or email).</p>
      <p><strong>4.3.</strong> To ensure the confidentiality of the Customer's personal data in accordance with the <a href="/legal/privacy/">Privacy Policy</a> and Federal Law No. 152-FZ "On Personal Data."</p>
      <p><strong>4.4.</strong> To notify the Customer of changes to Plans at least 14 calendar days before the changes take effect.</p>
      <p><strong>4.5.</strong> To review Customer complaints within 10 business days of receipt.</p>

      <p class="mt-3 mb-2"><strong>The Contractor has the right:</strong></p>
      <p><strong>4.6.</strong> To suspend service provision if the Customer violates the terms of this Agreement, the <a href="/legal/ai-policy/">Acceptable Use Policy</a>, or applicable laws of the Russian Federation.</p>
      <p><strong>4.7.</strong> To modify Plans with prior notice to the Customer. Changes do not apply to an already paid period.</p>
      <p><strong>4.8.</strong> To engage third parties for the performance of obligations under the Agreement (payment aggregators, CDN providers, AI model providers).</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">5. Rights and Obligations of the Customer</h2>

      <p class="mb-2"><strong>The Customer undertakes:</strong></p>
      <p><strong>5.1.</strong> To provide accurate data during registration.</p>
      <p><strong>5.2.</strong> To pay for services in accordance with the selected Plan.</p>
      <p><strong>5.3.</strong> To use the Service solely for lawful purposes and in accordance with the <a href="/legal/ai-policy/">Acceptable Use Policy</a>.</p>
      <p><strong>5.4.</strong> Not to share account credentials with third parties.</p>
      <p><strong>5.5.</strong> Not to use automated means to bypass Service restrictions (limits, quotas).</p>
      <p><strong>5.6.</strong> To independently label AI-generated content in accordance with legal requirements.</p>

      <p class="mt-3 mb-2"><strong>The Customer has the right:</strong></p>
      <p><strong>5.7.</strong> To choose any available Plan.</p>
      <p><strong>5.8.</strong> To cancel services at any time by deleting the account.</p>
      <p><strong>5.9.</strong> To use generated images for commercial purposes on paid Plans.</p>
      <p><strong>5.10.</strong> To request a refund in accordance with Section 8 of this Agreement.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">7. Liability of the Parties</h2>
      <p><strong>7.1.</strong> For non-performance or improper performance of obligations under this Agreement, the parties bear liability in accordance with the applicable laws of the Russian Federation.</p>
      <p class="mb-1"><strong>7.2.</strong> The Contractor is not liable for:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>The content of images generated based on the Customer's prompts;</li>
        <li>The Customer's infringement of third-party rights when using generated images;</li>
        <li>Inability to use the Service due to reasons beyond the Contractor's control (internet channel disruptions, third-party equipment failures, DDoS attacks);</li>
        <li>Actions of payment systems and banks.</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>7.3.</strong> The Contractor's aggregate liability for any claims is limited to the amount paid by the Customer for the last 12 months of Service use.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">8. Refunds</h2>
      <p><strong>8.1.</strong> The Customer may cancel paid services and request a refund within 14 calendar days of payment, provided that no more than 10% of the paid quota has been used.</p>
      <p class="mb-1"><strong>8.2.</strong> Refunds are not issued:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>After 14 days from the payment date;</li>
        <li>If more than 10% of the paid quota has been used;</li>
        <li>For the Free plan.</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>8.3.</strong> Refund requests should be sent to <a href="mailto:support@pixinlink.ru">support@pixinlink.ru</a> stating the invoice number and reason for the refund.</p>
      <p><strong>8.4.</strong> Refunds are processed using the same method as the original payment, within 30 calendar days of receiving the request.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">9. Confidentiality and Personal Data</h2>
      <p><strong>9.1.</strong> The Contractor processes the Customer's personal data in accordance with the <a href="/legal/privacy/">Privacy Policy</a> and Federal Law No. 152-FZ.</p>
      <p><strong>9.2.</strong> By accepting this offer, the Customer consents to the processing of their personal data to the extent and for the purposes specified in the Privacy Policy.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">10. Intellectual Property</h2>
      <p><strong>10.1.</strong> The PixInLink Service, its source code, design, documentation, and logos are the intellectual property of the Contractor.</p>
      <p class="mb-1"><strong>10.2.</strong> Exclusive rights to generated images:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>On paid Plans — transferred to the Customer in full, including the right to commercial use;</li>
        <li>On the Free plan — the Customer is granted a non-exclusive license for non-commercial use with mandatory preservation of the watermark.</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>10.3.</strong> The Customer warrants that the prompts they submit do not infringe the intellectual property rights of third parties.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">11. Dispute Resolution</h2>
      <p><strong>11.1.</strong> All disputes and disagreements arising from this Agreement shall be resolved through negotiations.</p>
      <p class="mb-1"><strong>11.2.</strong> A pre-trial claim procedure is mandatory. The response period for a claim is 10 business days. Claims are directed to:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>To the Contractor: <a href="mailto:legal@pixinlink.ru">legal@pixinlink.ru</a></li>
        <li>To the Customer: at the email address provided during registration.</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>11.3.</strong> If no agreement is reached, the dispute is submitted to the Moscow Commercial (Arbitrazh) Court (for legal entities and sole proprietors) or to a court of general jurisdiction at the Contractor's location (for individuals).</p>
      <p><strong>11.4.</strong> Governing law — the laws of the Russian Federation.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">12. Term and Termination</h2>
      <p><strong>12.1.</strong> The Agreement enters into force upon acceptance and remains in effect until the parties have fully performed their obligations.</p>
      <p><strong>12.2.</strong> The Customer may unilaterally terminate the Agreement by deleting their account. Any paid and unused period is not subject to refund (except as provided in Section 8).</p>
      <p class="mb-1"><strong>12.3.</strong> The Contractor may terminate the Agreement unilaterally in the following cases:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>Violation by the Customer of the terms of this Agreement;</li>
        <li>Violation of the <a href="/legal/ai-policy/">Acceptable Use Policy</a>;</li>
        <li>Non-payment for services within 30 days of the suspension date;</li>
        <li>Demand by government authorities.</li>
      </ul>
      <p><strong>12.4.</strong> Upon termination of the Agreement at the Contractor's initiative due to a violation by the Customer, funds paid for the unused period are not refunded.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">13. Miscellaneous</h2>
      <p><strong>13.1.</strong> This Agreement constitutes the entire agreement between the parties. All prior arrangements, correspondence, and oral assurances are void.</p>
      <p><strong>13.2.</strong> If any provision of the Agreement is held invalid, the remaining provisions shall continue in full force and effect.</p>
      <p><strong>13.3.</strong> The parties are released from liability for non-performance of obligations in the event of force majeure circumstances.</p>
    </div>
