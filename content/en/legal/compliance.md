      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">2. Federal Law 152-FZ on Personal Data</h2>
      <p class="mb-2">Federal Law No. 152-FZ is the primary law governing personal data processing in Russia. As a Russian company, we are required to comply — and we do.</p>

      <p class="mb-2"><strong>What this means for you:</strong></p>
      <ul>
        <li>Your data (email, name, prompts, images) is stored on servers in Russia</li>
        <li>We do not transfer your data to third parties without your consent — except where necessary to provide the service (payment system, cloud hosting)</li>
        <li>You can request a copy of your data, corrections, or deletion at any time</li>
        <li>You consent to data processing during registration — via a checkbox on the signup page</li>
        <li>In the event of a data breach, we will notify you and Roskomnadzor within 72 hours</li>
      </ul>

      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">3. Other Standards — Honestly</h2>

      <p class="mb-1"><strong>GDPR (European Union).</strong></p>
      <p class="mb-2">PixInLink does not target users in the EU and has no establishment in Europe — formally, GDPR does not apply to us. However, we respect its principles and use them as a guideline: data minimization, right to deletion, processing transparency. If you are in the EU and use our service, your data is processed on servers in Russia. Access, deletion, and export requests are handled through the same channels as under 152-FZ.</p>

      <p class="mb-1"><strong>Payment security.</strong></p>
      <p class="mb-0">We do not store bank card details. All payments are processed through certified payment systems (Sberbank PJSC) over secure channels. We receive only the transaction status — enough to display payment history in your dashboard.</p>
    </div>

    <div class="card paper pa-4 mb-4">
      <h2 class="use-text-title2 mb-3">4. Data Security</h2>
      <p class="mb-2">We apply reasonable measures to protect your data:</p>
      <ul>
        <li>All data is transmitted over <strong>HTTPS (TLS 1.3)</strong> — no one can intercept it in transit</li>
        <li>Data is stored encrypted at rest</li>
        <li>Server access is limited to authorized personnel, each with the minimum necessary privileges</li>
        <li>Regular backups — your data will not be lost in the event of hardware failure</li>
        <li>Round-the-clock security monitoring</li>
      </ul>
      <p class="mb-0" style="font-size:14px;color:#546e7a">We do not store full payment details (card number, CVV, expiration date) — these are processed directly by the payment system.</p>
